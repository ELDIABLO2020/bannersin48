# Custom Banner Website Structured Plan

## Working Concept

**Custom Banners Delivered in 48 Business Hours**

The website should be positioned as a focused, speed-first banner ordering platform for business, retail, schools, events, contractors, real estate, churches, sports teams, and personal celebrations.

The core promise:

> Order by 9 PM Monday–Saturday, approve your artwork, and get your custom banner delivered anywhere in the USA within 48 business hours.

The site should not feel like a general print shop. It should focus on one clear category: **custom vinyl banners**.

---

## Refined Business Positioning

### Primary Positioning

**We only do banners, so we do them faster and simpler than everyone else.**

### Primary Headline

**Custom Banners Delivered in 48 Business Hours**

### Subheadline

**Upload your artwork, design online, or let AI help you create a print-ready banner. Any size. Any color. One simple price.**

### Core Offer

- $4 per square foot
- $10 expedited shipping per unit
- 48-business-hour delivery across the USA
- Order cutoff: 9 PM Monday–Saturday
- 13 oz, 15 oz, and 18 oz vinyl options
- Grommets or pole pockets
- AI-assisted design and artwork checks

---

## What the Website Will Do

The website will allow customers to order custom banners quickly, either by uploading ready artwork, using a template, or designing with an online tool.

### Core Customer Flow

1. Choose banner size
2. Choose material
3. Choose finishing options
4. Upload artwork, use a template, or design online
5. Use AI to improve or check the design
6. Approve artwork
7. Checkout
8. Track order
9. Reorder or redesign from dashboard

The goal is to let a customer place a complete order in under 3 minutes if they already have artwork.

## Product Structure

## Banner Materials

### 13 oz Vinyl Banner

Best for standard indoor and outdoor banners.

### 15 oz Premium Vinyl Banner

Better durability and a more premium outdoor feel.

### 18 oz Heavy-Duty Blockout Banner

Premium option. This should be the only option available for double-sided printing.

---

## Finishing Options

Default finishing should be simple and production-safe:

- Hemmed edges
- Grommets every 2 feet
- Reinforced corners

Optional upgrades:

- No grommets
- Pole pockets: top, bottom, left, or right
- Wind slits
- Double-sided print on 18 oz material
- Custom grommet spacing

The interface should use simple visual selectors, not long dropdown lists.

---

|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| 2' × 4' | 8 | $32 | $10 | $42 |
| 2' × 6' | 12 | $48 | $10 | $58 |

| 3' × 6' | 18 | $72 | $10 | $82 |
| 3' × 8' | 24 | $96 | $10 | $106 |
| 3' × 12' | 36 | $144 | $10 | $154 |
| 4' × 6' | 24 | $96 | $10 | $106 |
| 4' × 8' | 32 | $128 | $10 | $138 |
| 5' × 8' | 40 | $160 | $10 | $170 |
| 5' × 10' | 50 | $200 | $10 | $210 |

### Pricing Formula

```text
Width in feet × height in feet × $4 + $10 shipping per unit
```

Example:

```text
3 ft × 8 ft = 24 sq ft
24 × $4 = $96
$96 + $10 shipping = $106 total
```

## High School Graduation Banner Engine

This is one of the strongest SEO and automation opportunities.

The website can generate landing pages for high school graduation banners across the United States.

Example page structure:

```text
/graduation-banners/texas/dallas/lincoln-high-school
/graduation-banners/florida/miami/coral-reef-high-school
/graduation-banners/california/los-angeles/venice-high-school
```

### Important Legal Guardrail

Do not automatically use official school logos, mascots, or trademarked artwork unless the school or customer uploads them and confirms usage rights.

Instead, use generic templates with:

- School name
- City and state
- Graduation year
- Student name
- Student photo upload
- Generic school color themes
- “Class of 2026”
- “Congrats Senior”

### Graduation Page Features

Each school landing page should include:

- School name
- City/state
- Graduation banner templates
- Upload student photo
- Add name/year
- 48-business-hour delivery guarantee
- Localized SEO copy
- Start design button

## 48-Business-Hour SLA Rules

The delivery promise should be strong but clearly controlled.

Recommended wording:

> Orders placed and print-approved before 9 PM Monday–Saturday qualify for 48-business-hour delivery.

The SLA timer should start only when:

1. Payment is captured
2. Artwork is approved
3. File passes print check
4. Shipping address is valid

If the customer uploads bad artwork and does not approve fixes, the SLA should pause.

### SLA Status Messages

The site should show clear order statuses:

- Qualifies for 48-business-hour delivery
- Needs artwork approval
- Cutoff missed — next production window starts tomorrow
- SLA paused: artwork issue
- In production
- Shipped
- Delivered

## Design Editor Recommendation

Do not build a Canva-style editor from scratch for the MVP unless the budget is large.

### Fastest Path

Use an embeddable web-to-print editor SDK.

Potential options:

- IMG.LY
- Design Huddle
- Customer's Canvas
- PitchPrint

The editor should support:

- Templates
- Image uploads
- Text editing
- Brand assets
- Print-safe zones
- PDF export

### Custom Path

A custom editor could use:

- Fabric.js or Konva
- PDF export engine
- AI layer
- Template engine
- Custom proofing workflow

This gives more control but creates more development risk.

# Final Recommendation

Build this as a **speed-first banner ordering platform**, not a full print marketplace.

The strongest angle is:

> Custom banners made simple, priced clearly, and delivered in 48 business hours.

The highest-value features are:

1. Fast quote/order flow
2. Upload artwork
3. Reorder dashboard
4. SLA countdown
5. Template library
6. AI print check
7. Graduation banner SEO pages

The AI design editor is valuable, but the real business moat is:

**Speed + simplicity + reorders + templates + guaranteed delivery.**
